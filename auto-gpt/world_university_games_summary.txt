The host city for the 2023 World University Games is Yekaterinburg. The World University Games, also known as the Universiade, is an international multi-sport event for university athletes. It is organized by the International University Sports Federation (FISU) and held every two years. The 2023 edition of the Games will feature various sports competitions, including athletics, swimming, basketball, and more. Yekaterinburg, located in Russia, will host the event and provide state-of-the-art facilities for the athletes.
